About Us

Welcome to Bharat Mart, your one-stop destination for the finest products and services, proudly founded by Ashish Kumar Singh and co-founded by Sougata Bhunia.

Our Story

Bharat Mart was born out of a shared passion for innovation and a deep commitment to providing quality solutions to the diverse needs of the Indian populace. Ashish Kumar Singh, with his visionary leadership, and Sougata Bhunia, with his relentless drive for excellence, came together with a common goal - to revolutionize the way India shops, connects, and thrives.

Our Vision

At Bharat Mart, our vision is to empower every Indian with access to top-notch products and services, irrespective of their location or background. We believe in bridging the urban-rural divide and ensuring that the benefits of technology and modern conveniences are accessible to all.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a marketplace that not only offers a wide array of products but also fosters a sense of community. We aim to create a platform where sellers, artisans, and entrepreneurs from every corner of India can showcase their talents and reach a wider audience.

Why Choose Bharat Mart?

Diverse Selection: We offer a curated selection of products, ranging from traditional handcrafted items to cutting-edge technology, ensuring that you find exactly what you're looking for. Quality Assurance: We prioritize quality and authenticity, working closely with sellers to ensure that every product meets our stringent standards. Empowering Sellers: By choosing Bharat Mart, you're supporting local businesses and artisans, contributing to the growth and prosperity of communities across India. User-Friendly Experience: Our user-friendly interface ensures that you can navigate our platform with ease, whether you're a tech-savvy urbanite or a first-time online shopper from a remote village. Customer-Centric Approach: Your satisfaction is our top priority. We are committed to providing excellent customer service and support throughout your shopping journey.

Join Us on this Journey

We invite you to be a part of our journey at Bharat Mart, where we celebrate the diversity, creativity, and resilience of India. Together, we can build a brighter future for our great nation, one transaction at a time. Thank you for choosing Bharat Mart as your trusted partner in discovering the best that India has to offer.
